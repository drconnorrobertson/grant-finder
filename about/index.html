About GrantFinder

Our mission is to connect nonprofits with the funding they need

Democratizing Grant Access

GrantFinder was founded on a simple belief: every nonprofit deserves equal access to funding opportunities, regardless of size or resources. We've created a centralized platform that eliminates the barriers to finding grants, making it easier for organizations to focus on their mission rather than spending countless hours searching for funding.

Many small and mid-sized nonprofits lack the dedicated grant research staff that larger organizations have. They struggle to navigate the fragmented landscape of federal, state, foundation, and corporate grants. GrantFinder levels the playing field by aggregating grant opportunities from thousands of sources into one searchable database.

We believe that access to funding information is a public good. By consolidating and organizing grant data, we help nonprofits discover opportunities they wouldn't have found otherwise, and we help funders connect with organizations that align with their values and goals.

Our Founder

GrantFinder was created by Dr. Connor Robertson, who saw firsthand how difficult it was for small nonprofits to compete for funding against larger, better-resourced organizations. With a background in data-driven strategy and a commitment to community development, Dr. Robertson set out to build a tool that would make grant discovery simple and accessible for everyone.

The platform is developed and maintained by Elixir Consulting Group, a technology consulting firm focused on building tools that create real-world impact. Elixir's team brings deep expertise in data engineering and user experience to ensure GrantFinder remains fast, accurate, and easy to use.

Our Core Values

Transparency

We believe in open access to grant data. All grant information on GrantFinder is publicly available and free to search, ensuring nonprofits can make informed decisions about where to apply.

Accuracy

Grant information is verified and regularly updated to reflect the latest deadlines, requirements, and funding amounts. We partner with funders and community contributors to maintain data integrity.

Accessibility

GrantFinder is free for all nonprofits. We're committed to ensuring that funding access is not limited by budget constraints, so our platform serves organizations of all sizes and types.
